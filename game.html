Modified Snake Game

About

To play the game you can click on the canvas above and either press "1" for single player or "2" for two players. The controls for single player are just the arrow keys, but for two players the second set of controls are WASD. The goal for single player mode is to try and grow your snake as long as possible by eating the circles. The goal for two players is each player has to fight for the circle and if one of the players hits their opponent's tail they lose, or if they hit the borders they also lose.

This was built with Processing another object oriented language and is built on Java. I used arraylists to create the snakes and the concept of polymorphism to create multiple shapes.
